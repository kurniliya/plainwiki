Internet Explorer does not natively support MathML

You are trying to view this page with Internet Explorer. Unfortunatelly this browser does not natively support MathML.

Do one of the following:

  • use MathPlayer plug-in:
  • use browser that natively supports MathML e.g.
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • SeaMonkey
    • Amaya
    • Opera not recommended
  • go to our main page http://www.primat.mephi.ru/

Technical details:

This section of our site is served with MIME type 'application/xml+xhtml'. That's why it can not be viewed with Internet Explorer without special plug-in.
